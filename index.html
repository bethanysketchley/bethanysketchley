About Me

Bethany Sketchley is an award-winning dramatic actor based right outside of Philadelphia, who is known to have an explosion of deep emotion in all her performances and reveals a specific understanding of the characters she plays. She has a background in jazz, modern, and contemporary dance. At the age of 4, she started taking lessons which sparked her love of storytelling and performing on stage. After experiencing the thrill and freedom of acting she never looked back!

Bethany has been awarded as Best Actress Grand Jury at the 2017 Playhouse West Philadelphia Annual Film Festival for the short film, "To Happy Endings" which she co-wrote and starred in. Recently, Bethany appeared on stage in Sean Grennan’s, The Tin Woman. When Bethany is not acting, she fills her time watching baking shows on YouTube, religiously watching Harry Potter movies, and learning Spanish!
